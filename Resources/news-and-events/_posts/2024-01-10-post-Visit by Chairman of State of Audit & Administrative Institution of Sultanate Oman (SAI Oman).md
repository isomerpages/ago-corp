<p>On 10 January 2024, His Excellency Sheikh Ghusan bin Hilal Al Alwai, Chairman
of State of Audit &amp; Administrative Institution of Sultanate Oman (SAI
Oman) visited AGO. He was accompanied by Mr Mohammad Al Harthy (Head of
Chairman’s Office), Mr Khalid Al Kharusi (Director General, Audit of Investments
and Companies), Mr Ahmad Al Mawali (Director General, Audit of Energy and
Minerals), Dr Hamyar Al Mahrouqi (Director, Training Center), Mr Yaqoub
Al Salmy (Director, Private Coordination), and Mr Anwar bin Ahmed Mohammed
Muqaibal (Charge d’affaires of the Sultanate of Oman’s Embassy to the Republic
of Singapore).</p>
<p>After a warm welcome by Auditor-General Goh Soon Poh, CPIB representatives
shared on CPIB’s role and work. This was followed by AGO representatives
sharing on various topics including AGO’s role, training, and digital transformation
journey.</p>
