<p>On 15 August 2024, the Auditor General (AG) of Malaysia, Datuk Wan Suraya
Wan Mohd Radzi, visited AGO. She was accompanied by the High Commissioner
of Malaysia to Singapore, Dato' Dr. Azfar Mohamad Mustafar, and Deputy
Auditor General of Malaysia (Performance Audit Sector) Datuk Nor Salwani
Muhammad.</p>
<p>After the Malaysian AG paid a call on Auditor-General Goh Soon Poh, AGO
officers made presentations on these topics: AGO’s digital transformation
journey, follow-the-dollar audits and AGO Academy. The Malaysian SAI also
shared with AGO on its SAI’s digital transformation journey.</p>
