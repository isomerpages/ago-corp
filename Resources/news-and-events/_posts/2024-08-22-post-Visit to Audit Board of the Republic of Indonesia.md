<p>On 22 August 2024, AG led a delegation to visit the Audit Board of the
Republic of Indonesia (BPK). The AGO delegation comprised AAG Mabel Watt,
Acting Director SPPD Candy Hong, Deputy Dean Academy Sabrina Fok, SAD Khoo
Wen Yong and AD James Koh.</p>
<p>During the visit, AG paid a courtesy call on the Chair of BPK, Dr Isma
Yatun. This was followed by BPK sharing with AGO on the its work, digital
transformation efforts, and manpower development and training initiatives.</p>
<p>As AGO will assume the ASEANSAI Chairmanship in 2025, BPK also shared
on some considerations in organising the ASEANSAI summit.</p>
<p>BPK also invited the AGO delegation to visit the ASEANSAI Secretariat
Office located within the BPK headquarters office in Jakarta.</p>
<p>The visit signifies the close relationship between the two SAIs.</p>
