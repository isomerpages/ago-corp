<p>On 4 February 2025, AGO hosted a delegation from the Audit Board of the
Republic of Indonesia (BPK). The delegation was led by Dr Isma Yatun, Chair
of BPK. Other members of the delegation were Director Mr Teguh Widodo,
Deputy Director Ms Ami Rahmawati, Senior Auditor Mr Maltreba Rufosasdo
Kispatelo and Analyst Ms Yuli Puspitasari.</p>
<p>The visit commenced with a courtesy call by Dr Yatun on AG. AGO shared
with the delegation on AGO's role and work, and how AGO utilises data in
procurement audits. BPK then shared on its knowledge management framework.</p>
<p>Both SAIs also took the opportunity to discuss preparations for the upcoming
ASEANSAI Summit.</p>
<p>The meeting was insightful and both SAIs gained valuable insights from
one another.</p>
