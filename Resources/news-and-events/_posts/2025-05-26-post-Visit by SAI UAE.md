<p>On 26 May 2025, His Excellency Humaid Abushibs, Chairman of the United
Arab Emirates Accountability Authority (SAI UAE), visited AGO. He was accompanied
by His Excellency Wael Mahmoud (Director General of Audit Sector), Ms Hanan
Al Marzouqi (Director – Audit Sector), Mr Amer Al Menhali (Deputy Director
– Asia, Arab and GCC Relations Department) and Ms Alia Al Shamsi (Chairman
Secretary of Strategic Relations Affairs).</p>
<p>AG Wai Choong warmly welcomed the delegation. Acting Director Candy shared
on AGO’s role and its work. This was followed by SAI UAE’s sharing on its
work. After the presentations, the two SAIs had an engaging exchange on
the adoption of technology in audit work.</p>
