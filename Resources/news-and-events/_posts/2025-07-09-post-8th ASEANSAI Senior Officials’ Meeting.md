<p>Assistant Auditor-General Mabel Watt led an AGO delegation to attend the
8th ASEANSAI Senior Officials’ Meeting (SOM) in Manila from 9 to 10 July
2025. The Commission on Audit of the Republic of Philippines, who is the
ASEANSAI Chair for 2023- 2025, hosted the biennial SOM, while the ASEANSAI
Secretariat chaired the Meeting.</p>
<p>Key agenda items endorsed at the meeting included the ASEANSAI Strategic
Plan 2026–2029, proposed amendments to the ASEANSAI Rules and Procedures,
and the ASEANSAI training frameworks.</p>
<p>The agenda items discussed at SOM would subsequently be tabled for approval
at the 8th ASEANSAI Summit in November 2025, which would be hosted by AGO
as the incoming ASEANSAI Chair for 2025-2027.</p>
