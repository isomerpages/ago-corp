<p>On 10 March 2026, AGO welcomed a delegation from Thailand's Office of
the National Anti-Corruption Commission (ONACC), led by Mr Tawichat Nillakan,
Deputy Secretary-General (Region 9). Director Paul Loke hosted the visit.</p>
<p>The meeting began with a presentation by Paul on AGO's transformation
journey. The knowledge sharing was reciprocated by the ONACC delegation,
which also shared their digital transformation experience.</p>
<p>The visit allowed both parties to gain valuable perspectives on leveraging
digital transformation to enhance work in their respective domains.</p>
